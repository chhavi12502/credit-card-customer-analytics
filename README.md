# 💳 Credit Card Customer Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze credit card transactions, customer behavior, and business performance. The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into actionable business insights through dynamic visualizations and KPI reporting.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The objective of this project is to help financial institutions:

- Monitor overall business performance
- Analyze customer spending behavior
- Identify high-value customer segments
- Track revenue and transaction trends
- Support data-driven decision making

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- DAX
- Power Query
- Microsoft Excel

---

## 📊 Key KPIs

- Total Revenue
- Total Transaction Amount
- Interest Earned
- Customer Income
- Customer Satisfaction Score (CSS)

---

## 📈 Dashboard Highlights

- Revenue Analysis by Quarter
- Revenue by Card Category
- Revenue by Occupation
- Revenue by Education Level
- Revenue by Expense Type
- Customer Demographics
- Weekly Revenue Trend
- Revenue by State
- Interactive Filters & Slicers

---

## 💡 Business Insights

- Blue Card customers generated the highest revenue.
- Business professionals contributed the highest customer revenue.
- Graduate customers formed the highest revenue-generating education segment.
- Bills and Entertainment were the major spending categories.
- Revenue remained strong across all quarters with Quarter 4 showing the highest performance.
